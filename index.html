About Me

I'm a passionate doodle artist with over 5 years of experience creating playful and imaginative illustrations. My work is characterized by whimsical lines, quirky characters, and a touch of humor.

I believe that doodling is more than just drawing - it's a way to communicate ideas, tell stories, and bring joy to people's lives. My mission is to spread creativity and positivity through my art.

When I'm not doodling, you can find me drinking coffee, exploring nature for inspiration, or teaching workshops to aspiring artists.
